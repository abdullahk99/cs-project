  • How do I change my password?

    Password changing can be only done if/when a student has had a change in his student id number. Contacting your guidence councoler or home room teacher is a very good idea.

  • How do I sign up?

    As you launch this app, you will already have a student id number as well as a birth date as your password. Use these items to log in and sign up!

  • Can I remove a post?

    To remove an unwanted post, simply unfollow the teacher/club. This will allow you to not view anymore of the annoucments posted from the spesific category.

  • How can I contact a club/teacher?

    Contacting a teacher over this app is not an avaliable feauture, as personal messaging between students and clubs/teachers has not as of yet been created. However, look for the contact name in a post and be sure to look around school and find that teacher/club!
